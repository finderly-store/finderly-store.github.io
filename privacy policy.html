Information We Collect

  • Collection for Security: We collect IP addresses to help detect and prevent spam or abuse of our service.

ZIP Code for Service Direct Affiliation

  • Affiliation with Service Direct: We have partnered with Service Direct to help users find and hire home improvement service providers. If we detect that you are interested in home improvement services while using the App, we may ask for your ZIP code.
  • Purpose: Your ZIP code is used solely to determine the most relevant service provider phone number in your area.
  • Display of Phone Number: Once we have your ZIP code, we will display a phone number through which you can contact and hire a local home improvement service provider.
  • Limited Use and Retention: We do not store your ZIP code for any purpose other than enabling this feature. We also do not share your ZIP code with any other parties except as needed to provide the relevant phone number.
